# Kubernetes Security and Compliance with Kubescape
## Problem:
- Lack of Visibility into Potential Security Issues: Many Kubernetes clusters operate without sufficient visibility into security vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and compliance violations. This blind spot makes it challenging for teams to detect and address risks before they escalate, leaving clusters exposed to attacks and compliance failures.
- Risk of Security Breaches: Unpatched vulnerabilities and insecure configurations within kubernetes clusters can result in data leaks, service disruptions, and security breaches.

## Solution:
- Enhanced Security Visibility: Kubescape provides comprehensive insights into Kubernetes clusters by continuously scanning for vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and compliance gaps. Its detailed dashboards and reports ensure teams are fully aware of potential security issues, enabling proactive risk management and decision-making.
- Compliance with Industry Standards: By integrating with frameworks like the NSA-CISA Kubernetes Hardening Guide and CIS Kubernetes Benchmark, Kubescape ensures adherence to security best practices.
- Actionable Remediation: Provides detailed, actionable steps to resolve identified issues, simplifying vulnerability fixes and compliance management.
